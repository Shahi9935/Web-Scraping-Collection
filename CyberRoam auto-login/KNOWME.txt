CyberRoam auto-login
By running this script, your browser will open automatically on cyberRoam page and you will be logged in.
Firstly edit the main.py by making following changes:
1) Enter your username(Roll no.) in between quotes on line 14
2) Enter your password in between quotes on line 15
You are done!. Now double click on "main.py" file everytime you want to login.
Requirements - python with selenium, gecko driver in path, firefox browser, LAN connection in BIT Mesra and a CyberRoam ID and password.
